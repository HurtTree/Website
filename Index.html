CHARGE About Contact

CHARGE is a lovely little retro platformer that I have been working on for the past few months. It features 2-bit colors with interchangable palettes, over 100 levels, and a very brave little battery. I always enjoyed older platformer games when I was young, so I wanted to try and make my own! My planned release platforms are Steam, Xbox, Microsoft Store, and soon after launch, Nintendo Switch.

If you would like to support this project, I have launched a KICKSTARTER campaign for it! You can help make my dreams come true and get a discount on the game along with other exclusive rewards!

About

My name is Christian and I am an aspiring game developer. I have worked on small hobbyist projects and modding ventures over the past few years, but now I am looking to make something a bit different. Currently my passion project is a neat little game called CHARGE! I hope that someday people enjoy playing my games just as much as I enjoy playing other's games.
